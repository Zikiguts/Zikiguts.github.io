About Me

   Hello! Looks like you found my portfolio! Let me introduce myself. My name is Ezekiel Gutierrez, and if that's hard to pronounce you can just call me Zeke! I have a passion for learning new things, and spend most my free time studying programming, languages, art, and music!

   Since childhood I've always wanted to be a software engineer. As a 5 year old I distinctly remember standing in awe behind my older brother as I watched him program in MS QuickBasic. I began reading books on programming before I even had a computer that I could use to code with. When I eventually had access to a computer for a few hours in the evenings, I would do everything to make the most of that time.

   Over the years I've written hundreds of programs that have taught me many different programming related skills. I'm most satisfied when I have a deep foundational understanding of something from the lowest level up.

   I'm currently looking for an interesting low level programming job or something in game development where I can put my passion to work and express my creativity!
