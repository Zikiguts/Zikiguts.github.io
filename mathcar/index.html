Mathcar

  Mathcar is a unique and exciting racing game that combines the thrill of competition with the challenge of math education. This cross-platform game can be played on Windows and Linux and allows players to race against themselves or against other players. The twist is that the speed of the car is controlled by solving math problems correctly and quickly. This game provides an engaging and effective way to improve math fact skills, and the single and multiplayer options give players the flexibility to choose their preferred mode of play. Whether you're looking to improve your math skills or just want a fun and competitive racing game, Mathcar is your speedway to success. Credits to D.J. Peters for his FBSound Library.
