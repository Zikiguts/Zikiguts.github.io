Introductory Piano Lessons

Technique

Ezekiel introduces students to a healthy and efficient piano technique that minimizes tension to help students avoid pain or injury from poor technique.

Music Theory

Ezekiel teaches the fundamentals of notation and music theory to start new pianists off on the right foot. By leaning the language of music, students are more able to play beautifully and expressively.

Aural Skills

Ezekiel can help students develop their musical hearing by teaching traditional solfege. This helps them to play better by ear and also aids with remembering music.
