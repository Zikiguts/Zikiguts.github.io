Meet your Teacher


Ezekiel Gutierrez, Musician


   Ezekiel Gutierrez began his journey as a musician studying classical guitar as a teenager. However, as an adult, like many other musicians, he found himself pursuing other careers in order to be successful. He worked jobs in retail management, software development, the US Navy, and security but eventually realized that more than anything he wanted to play music. He is now working as a substitute teacher in Talihina while pursuing music theory, composition, piano and voice at Eastern Oklahoma State College.

   Ezekiel offers private instruction on the piano and fundamental music theory concepts. As a music teacher he enjoys being able to guide his students in developing the skills and proficiencies required to make beautiful music!

   Besides music Ezekiel enjoys running, reading books and writing computer software.
