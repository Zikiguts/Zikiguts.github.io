To Face The Bullets

  "To Face the Bullets" is a strategy game with action combat centered around a fictional nation's war and revolution. The game features a simple custom 2D physics model for the aircraft and ground vehicles with gravity, lift, friction, and drag. It also has a highly customizable particle effects engine. Credits to D.J. Peters for his FBSound and FBTrueType libraries.
